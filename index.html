ABOUT
US

Osho Madhushala is a place for meditation and where people can meet their true selves. Osho Madhushala was founded in 1980 by swami Chandermoli Jii. The Osho Madhushala Foundation is licensed under Osho International. Here we organize daily sessions from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM for osho meditations. Currently Osho Madhushala is lead by Swami Aantar Muni. We do also organise some perodic festivals for meditation purposes with daily meditation.

If you want to live a more fulfilled life, first you will want to know your potential, who you really are. Meditation is the route to that knowing. It is the methodology of the science of awareness. Daily Meditation

1h | ₹1,000

Many meditative techniques require one to sit still and silent. But for most of us accumulated stress in our bodymind makes that difficult. Before we can hope to access our inner powerhouse of consciousness, we need to let go of our tensions. Thereby we have introduced meditation sessions on daily bases, which will let you live to the fullest of yourself.
